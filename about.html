About Us

We are Year 9 Morado students who are passionate about caring for the environment and improving our community. As young learners, we believe that technology can be a powerful tool to inspire change. By creating this website, we want to help people discover the beauty of Yangon's parks while also sharing simple, meaningful ways to protect them.

Why we choose this topic?

We chose this topic because Yangon's parks are important places for families, students, and the community to relax, exercise, and connect with nature. However, many people may not know how to take care of these green spaces or understand the environmental challenges they face. We wanted to create something that helps visitors learn more about sustainability while also appreciating the natural beauty around them.

About Us

Each member of our team brings different strengths-research, design, creativity, writing, coding, and problem-solving. Together, we combined our ideas to build a website that is informative, easy to use, and enjoyable for all visitors. Our goal is to raise awareness, encourage eco-friendly habits, and help create a greener, healthier future for everyone.
